Tech Career — United States
Dec 2025 — Apr 2026
San Francisco, CA
Stealth AI Startup
Cofounder — CRO

Co-founded a data licensing platform sourcing compliant enterprise datasets for AI model training, from hospital systems across Latin America and India to conversations with frontier labs. Cold-starting a company from nothing is mostly an exercise in earning trust for something that does not exist yet.

What I took from it: in the earliest days, conviction and compliance matter more than product polish.

Sept 2024 — Oct 2025
San Francisco, CA
Reka AI
Global Sales & Partnerships Lead  |  Series A ($180M)

Joined a Series A multimodal AI company as one of the first commercial hires, with no sales infrastructure to inherit. I built the go-to-market motion from scratch: co-sell frameworks with NVIDIA, Dell, HPE, Qualcomm and ARM, and the first enterprise pilots for vision and speech agents across robotics, drones and autonomous systems. Most of the work was translating what the research team could do into something a buyer could act on.

What I took from it: selling frontier technology is mostly translation, and the market forgives a rough product long before it forgives a story it cannot follow.

July 2023 — Mar 2024
Milpitas, CA
Renesas Electronics
Key Account Manager — Apple

Managed the Apple account at a leading semiconductor company, working design-ins across AirPods, Vision Pro, Watch and Beats through multi-quarter cycles from evaluation to mass production. This is where I learned how the physical layer of technology actually ships.

What I took from it: enterprise hardware moves at the speed of trust and engineering cadence, not sales quarters.

June 2022 — June 2023
Folsom, CA
Micron Technology
Business Development Intern (Part-time) & Industrial Segment Marketing Intern

Two roles in business development and industrial marketing, spent tracking where robotics, autonomous vehicles and urban air mobility were heading, in conversation with Zoox, Waymo, Rivian, Lucid and Tesla. My first close look at the semiconductor demand behind physical AI.

What I took from it: being early to a market is a research discipline long before it is a sales one.

Early Career — India

Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Flamingo Industries
Founder
Best Energy Startup — AEEE 2018 Top 10 Cleantech — GCIP USA 2018 D-Labs Accelerator — ISB India 2018 Patent: Hot Air Recirculation in Furnaces

Founded and ran a thermal engineering and manufacturing company for nearly a decade. Filed a patent, built a team of 25+ engineers, and grew to 150+ enterprise accounts including BASF, Mitsubishi and Merck. This is where I learned to build a business with no playbook and no outside funding.

What I took from it: everything downstream of a sale, delivery, service and retention, is where a reputation is actually earned.
