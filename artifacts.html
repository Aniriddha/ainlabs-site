AI Chatbot · Infographics
Walk the Talk AI Booking Bot
Indiana Wesleyan University, Machine Learning Course
Objective

To practice deep interviewing, AI prompt engineering, and AI system design by building a working Speaker's Bureau booking bot as a graded team project.

Process

Interviewed teammates to uncover their passions beyond the resume, used generative AI to write rousing TED Talk introductions and professional speaker profiles, compiled the profiles into a knowledge base, and built a booking agent in Google AI Studio with a strict system prompt grounded only in that data. The team then produced the marketing and process infographics entirely with AI tools and demonstrated the bot live in class with unscripted audience queries.

Tools

Google AI Studio, generative AI assistants for interview synthesis and speaker profiles, AI image generation for both infographics.

Value Proposition

Proves hands-on skill in prompt engineering, grounding a chatbot in custom data, and pairing a working AI product with AI-generated marketing, from discovery interviews to a live demo.
